The Artist

Brown-Eyes White Boy

Self-described "14yo austrian rapartist" from Salzburg. Released two EPs in early 2018 on SoundCloud and Bandcamp, both of which have since been deleted. 10 of 15 tracks survived on the Archive (1619) compilation in 2020. Of the remaining 5, three have been partially recovered (YouTube uploads, slowed+reverb edits), but 2 tracks — Lola Bunny and ea7 — remain completely lost with no known audio.

Can You Help Find the Originals?

2 tracks are completely lost. 3 more exist only in degraded or alternate forms (YouTube uploads, slowed+reverb edits). If you downloaded or saved any of these songs in original quality between February and November 2018, please reach out:

  • SoundCloud offline downloads or cache files from 2018
  • Bandcamp purchases or downloads (album ID: 853452325 or 1387501607)
  • Screen recordings or phone recordings of the tracks playing
  • YouTube reuploads, reaction videos, or playlists that included BEWB tracks
  • Old Discord messages, Telegram chats, or file shares with .mp3/.wav links
  • Cached files on old phones, laptops, or external drives
